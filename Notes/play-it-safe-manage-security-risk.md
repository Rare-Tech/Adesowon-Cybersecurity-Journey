# 🔐 Play It Safe: Manage Security Risk

## 📚 Course Overview

This course (part of the Google Cybersecurity Certificate on Coursera) focuses on how organizations identify, assess, and manage cybersecurity risks to protect systems, data, and users.

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts

### 🔹 Risk Management

* Risk = Threat × Vulnerability × Impact
* The goal is not to eliminate risk, but to reduce it to an acceptable level
* Security must balance protection with business operations

---

### 🔹 Assets, Threats, and Vulnerabilities

* **Assets**: Valuable resources (data, systems, people)
* **Threats**: Potential dangers (hackers, malware, insider threats)
* **Vulnerabilities**: Weaknesses that can be exploited

---

### 🔹 CIA Triad

A core principle of cybersecurity:

* **Confidentiality** – Protect sensitive information from unauthorized access
* **Integrity** – Ensure data is accurate and unchanged
* **Availability** – Ensure systems and data are accessible when needed

---

### 🔹 Security Frameworks

Organizations use frameworks to manage security risks:

* NIST Cybersecurity Framework
* ISO/IEC 27001

These frameworks help standardize security practices and improve risk management.

---

### 🔹 Risk Assessment Process

1. Identify assets
2. Identify threats and vulnerabilities
3. Assess risk (likelihood and impact)
4. Prioritize risks
5. Recommend security controls

---

### 🔹 Security Controls

Measures used to reduce risk:

* **Administrative** – Policies, procedures, training
* **Technical** – Firewalls, encryption, antivirus
* **Physical** – Locks, surveillance systems

---

### 🔹 Compliance and Ethics

* Organizations must follow legal and regulatory requirements
* Ethical responsibility includes protecting user data and respecting privacy

---
